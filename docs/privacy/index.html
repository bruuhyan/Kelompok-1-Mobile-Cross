TrustEnd Privacy Policy

TrustEnd is a workforce attendance and organization management app. This policy explains how TrustEnd handles data used for accounts, attendance validation, trust scores, requests, reports, tasks, and supervisor review.

Last updated: June 6, 2026

Data We Collect

TrustEnd collects account information such as name, email address, role, organization membership, profile photo, account status, and account creation/update timestamps.

TrustEnd also stores attendance records, holiday/overtime requests, employee reports, report photos, task assignments, task submissions, trust score information, supervisor review activity, and organization settings.

Location and Network Data

When users check in or check out, TrustEnd collects precise GPS coordinates, local network information such as WiFi SSID/BSSID when available, and local IP address. This data is used to validate attendance against organization rules and may flag records for supervisor review.

TrustEnd does not request background location access. Location is requested when users perform attendance or location setup actions.

Photos and User Content

Users may upload profile pictures and report photos. Users may also submit reports, task notes, request reasons, and related workplace content.

How We Use Data

Data is used to authenticate users, manage organizations, validate attendance, calculate trust scores, support supervisor approvals, handle reports and requests, assign/review tasks, and improve reliability of the app experience.

Sharing

Data is shared only within the user's organization according to role permissions. Supervisors and admins can review employee attendance, requests, reports, tasks, and trust score information. TrustEnd does not sell personal data.

Retention and Deletion

Organization records are retained while the account is active or as needed for workplace audit purposes. Users can request account and data deletion from inside the app. Some records may be retained when required for security, legal, or organizational audit reasons.

Contact

For privacy or account deletion requests, contact support.trustend@gmail.com.

TrustEnd Account Deletion

TrustEnd users can request deletion of their account and related data from inside the app.

Last updated: June 6, 2026

How to Request Deletion in the App

  1. Sign in to TrustEnd.
  2. Open Profile.
  3. Tap Request Account Deletion.
  4. Add an optional reason.
  5. Submit the request.

What May Be Deleted

Deletion may include the user's profile, profile photo, report photos, organization membership, attendance records, requests, reports, task submissions, and related user-generated content.

What May Be Retained

Some records may be retained if required for workplace audit, fraud prevention, security, legal compliance, or organization recordkeeping. Retained records may be anonymized where appropriate.

Processing Time

Requests are reviewed before completion. For urgent deletion requests or questions, contact support.trustend@gmail.com.
